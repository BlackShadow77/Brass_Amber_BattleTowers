Stone chests
-collectible
-drops 8 chest shards [1 for each type of chest]
-shards craft into a chest
-a golem eye placed in the matching chest makes a golem chest
-stone chest is lava proof but not blast proof

Golem chests
-blast/lava proof
-crafted with 8 shards and a corresponding golem eye [land eye for stone chests]
-double golem chest at the top of the tower drops 16 shards and 2 eyes
